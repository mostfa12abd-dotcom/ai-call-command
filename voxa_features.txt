# Voxa AI Call Center - Features & Capabilities

Below is a comprehensive list of features that Voxa AI Call Center provides (or will provide). You can use this as a system prompt or knowledge base for your VAPI assistant.

## Core Features (Currently Active)
1. **AI Inbound & Outbound Calling:** 
   Provide instant, human-like voice responses to customers 24/7 without wait times.
2. **Live Analytics Dashboard:** 
   A real-time dashboard that tracks total calls, missed calls, and average call durations for each business.
3. **Sentiment Analysis:** 
   Automatically detects the customer's mood (Happy, Neutral, Angry) during the call and logs it for review.
4. **Auto-Transcription & Summarization:** 
   The system generates a full text transcript of every call, along with a two-word quick summary and a detailed paragraph summary.
5. **Smart Customer CRM:** 
   Automatically saves caller details (Name, Phone Number, Call History) into a unified customer directory.

## Automated Actions & Marketing Integrations
6. **WhatsApp Follow-ups:** 
   Immediately after a call ends, the system can trigger automated WhatsApp messages (e.g., payment links, confirmation details, or thank-you notes) via n8n workflows.
7. **Proactive Lead Outreach & Sales:** 
   The AI can proactively reach out to leads, follow up with potential clients, and promote products or services through natural, human-like conversations designed to convert.

## Tailored Digital Agency Services
8. **Fully Tailored Growth Systems:** 
   We do not use one-size-fits-all solutions. Every AI and marketing strategy is fully customized based on the client's industry, goals, and target audience.
9. **High-Performance Web Development:** 
   Design and development of websites built specifically to attract, engage, and convert visitors into customers.
10. **End-to-End Social Media Management:** 
    Comprehensive management of social platforms including content creation, branding, campaign execution, and audience growth.

## Advanced Capabilities
11. **Smart Appointment Scheduling:** 
    The AI assistant can read the company's calendar (e.g., Google Calendar) and book, cancel, or reschedule appointments directly during the call.
12. **Custom Data Extraction:** 
    The AI can ask specific questions (like Order ID, Medical Symptoms, or ID numbers) and extract them into neatly organized data fields on the dashboard.
13. **Knowledge Base Q&A:** 
    Upload company documents (menus, return policies, FAQs) and the AI will accurately answer customer questions based exclusively on that data.
14. **Intelligent Call Transfer:** 
    If a customer is extremely angry or has a complex issue, the AI can seamlessly route the call to a human agent.
15. **Multi-language Support:** 
    The AI can automatically detect the caller's language and respond fluently in languages like English, Arabic, Spanish, etc.
